Team Registration

Teams (4 to 6 students) that wish to compete in the MOAA can register by completing the following steps:

  1. Fill out the Registration Form at this link.
  2. Have each student and one of their parents or legal guardians sign, scan, and send the competition waiver to the MOAA team.
  3. Click the button below to pay the $48 registration fee through PayPal or any credit card. Be sure that the name of your team matches on the Registration Form and on the payment box below. Teams that require financial assistance to attend the competition may reach out to the MOAA team before registering - financial aid will be provided on a case-by-case basis.
That's it - you're done! Be sure to save your payment receipt. Registration will close at 11:59 AM EST on September 30th, 2018. Teams will be registered on a first-come, first-serve basis.

Individual Registration

Students can attend if they are in 8th Grade or below. Schools may register as many teams as they wish, each with at most 6 students. Individual participants are also more than welcome to register. We recommend 4 to 6 members per team, so individuals and teams with less than 4 students may be combined for certain rounds of the competition.

What

is the MOAA?

The Math Open At Andover is an annual mathematics competition for middle school teams, hosted by Phillips Academy Andover in Andover, Massachusetts. At MOAA, middle school students can experience mathematical exploration, collaboration, and competition. We hope to promote the involvement of middle school students in the mathematical community with a fulfilling, intellectually stimulating, and fun experience for everybody.
The problems and formatting of MOAA are inspired by the most memorable competition questions that we have encountered in our own journeys through competition mathematics. Attending the MOAA competition is more than a standalone event - it is also designed to prepare students for other high school contests and give them a valuable taste of the mathematics that may await them in the future. As the organizers of the MOAA, we are passionate mathematicians and we would love to show you a good time at Phillips Academy Andover!

Create Virtual Study Groups

The key to learning is to work with peers and form study groups. If you are struggling to find a compatible study partner, School Ground is perfect for you.

Easier Learning Experience

With School Ground, you will be able to study more effectively with collaborations with fellow students. Additionally, you can post questions in your study group forum at any time, and even request help from knowledgeable tutors who have already taken your course.

Connect With New Classmates

Collaboration is becoming more popular as people realize how effective studying with others is. Students in any grade will be able to reach out to one other for study help, and you will get a chance to meet new classmates you did know before.

Features

By creating a study group with School Ground, you gain access to many powerful, accessable, and user-friendly functionality.

  • Share Files

    You can upload, share, and access anything within a study group, including study notes, images, videos, documents, and other files.

  • Ask and Answer Questions

    Post any questions you have on a question forum, whether you want to learn seomthing new or you need help on a homework problem.

  • Virtual Classroom

    Using breathtaking API's, School Ground will allow you to enter a virtual classroom, where you can write on a shared whiteboard, video call others, and discuss problems.

  • Tutors

    If you need help, you can easily reach out to peer tutors. Otherwise, you can volunteer to tutor others to gain glory points and prizes for our tutor of the month award!

  • Schedule Study Sessions

    You can schedule public or private in-person study sessions. This is a great way to create an open study group with everyone in your class right before an exam!

  • Chat

    Enter live chat groups with your study partners and friends, or schedule a live chat with a tutor if you have a quick question.
